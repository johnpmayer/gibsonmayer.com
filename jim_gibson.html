James J. Gibson, CPA, PFS

Email

James J. Gibson, CPA is an easygoing individual who works well with people. His experience includes providing accounting, tax, and consulting services for individuals, partnerships, and corporations. Additionally, Mr. Gibson provides estate and financial planning services and has been active in the financial services market for nearly thirty years.

Jim's experience provides the benefit of having become closely involved in many complex problems in real estate, construction, manufacturing and various service companies. He has successfully presented many cases for a variety of clients before the Internal Revenue Service.

In 1983 Mr. Gibson received his Bachelor of Science degree from the Philadelphia College of Textiles and Science. He developed his background and experience with specialization in taxation with local certified public accounting firms and Pannel Kerr Forster Worldwide CPA Firm. He is currently a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and Pennsylvania Institute of Certified Public Accountants and is a member of the local Chamber of Commerce.
