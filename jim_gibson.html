James J. Gibson, CPA, PFS

Email

Seen here greeting a client, Jim is an easygoing individual who works well with people. You can drop off or mail your tax information for us to process. If we need additional information we'll call you. If we need to see you in the office we'll set up an appointment to discuss your situation.

Once done, your returns are organized in an easy to read binder format with clear instructions on where to sign and pre-addressed envelopes are provided for your convenience. Copies of your returns (Multi State Returns are no problem) are also included so the binder can be stored for future use.

James J. Gibson, CPA provides the benefit of having become closely involved in many complex problems of clients in real estate, construction, manufacturing and various service companies.

He has presented successfully many cases for a variety of clients before the Internal Revenue Service.

His experience includes providing accounting, tax, and consulting services for individuals, partnerships, and corporations.

Additionally, Mr. Gibson provides estate and financial planning services and has been active in the financial services market for nearly thirty years.

In 1983, Mr. Gibson received a Bachelor of Science degree from the Philadelphia College of Textiles and Science. He developed his background and experience with specialization in taxation with local certified public accounting firms and Pannel Kerr Forster Worldwide CPA Firm. He is currently a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and Pennsylvania Institute of Certified Public Accountants and is a member of the local Chamber of Commerce.
