John P. Mayer, CPA

Email

John is a hard working CPA who is, at your request, often at your place of business helping you with the challenging needs of commerce today. Installing accounting software & providing training so you can run your own business, is just one of his many talents. His personality reflects how easy it will be to work with him.

John P. Mayer, CPA brings nearly thirty years of current public accounting experience to the organization with a focus on accounting, auditing, tax, and consulting services. His clients include technology companies, research and development, manufacturering, real estate development, and professional service companies. Prior to entering public accounting, he was a member of the management team of a large international food processing company.

In 1984, Mr. Mayer received his Bachelor of Science degrees in Business Management and Accountancy from St. Peter's College, Jersey City, NJ. He is currently licensed as a Certified Public Accountant in both the State of New Jersey and the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, and is a member of the American Institute of Certifed Public Accountants, the New Jersey Society of Certified Public Accountants, and the Pennsylvania Institute of Certified Public Accountants.
